FUN FACT_01


It only takes 62 miles of traveling straight up to reach outer space. Going highway speeds in a rocketship could hypothetically there in about an hour.

While space is considerably proximal, driving a car to the nearest star at highway speeds -- 70 mph -- would take more than 356 billion years.

FUN FACT_04

Gravity has an infinite range, with its strength weakening as distance increases, but never reaching zero. That means that, everything in the universe with any mass at all is pulling on you, right now.

FUN FACT_05

Massive objects curve space[time]. Time is planar, so the closer you are to a massive object, the more the curvature in the fabric. Because of this, time is actually warped. Think of a heavy ball on a flexible rubber sheet.
