FUN FACT_01

It only takes 62 miles of traveling straight up to reach outer space. Going highway speeds in a rocketship could hypothetically there in about an hour.





FUN FACT_02

Our Sun accounts for 99.86% of the total mass within our solar system. Given that math, all of the planets, asteriods, moons, and other matter account for the remaining 0.14%.





FUN FACT_03

Gravity has an infinite range, with its strength weakening as distance increases, but never reaching zero. That means that, right now, everything in the universe with any mass at all is pulling on you, right now.





FUN FACT_04

Massive objects curve space[time]. Time is planar, so the closer you are to a massive object, the more the curvature in the fabric. Because of this, time is actually warped by gravity and mass -- think of a heavy ball on a flexible rubber sheet.
